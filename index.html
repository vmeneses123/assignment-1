Who is Lana del Rey?

Lana Del Rey, whose real name is Elizabeth Woolridge Grant, is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. Her music is known for its cinematic quality and exploration of tragic romance, glamour, and melancholia.

Early Life and Career

Lana Del Rey was born June 21, 1985, Manhattan, New York, U.S. Lana Del Rey began her career performing at open mic nights and clubs, and releasing demos. She signed with an independent record label after entering a songwriting competition in 2006.

Lana's Rise to Fame

Lana Del Rey rose to fame in 2011 with the viral success of her song "Video Games". The song was the first single from her debut album, Born to Die, which was released in 2012.
